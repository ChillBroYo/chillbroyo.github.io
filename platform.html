Our Campaign Priorities

Building a stronger, fairer District 4 with community‑centered solutions.

Housing Opportunities

  • Strengthen tenant protections and eviction defense.
  • Support pathways to homeownership for longtime residents.
  • Uphold Community Plans shaped by residents — not developers.
  • Commit to affirmatively furthering fair housing citywide.
  • End policies that deepen segregation.

Public Safety Rooted in Community Trust

  • Community‑based policing with consistent neighborhood officers.
  • Police accountability and cultural competency training.
  • Safer streets, lighting, and traffic calming.
  • Youth mentorship and violence prevention programs.
  • Mental health crisis response teams.

Infrastructure That Serves the People

  • Equity‑driven capital budgeting.
  • Sidewalks, lighting, drainage, and park investment.
  • Climate resilience and flood prevention.
  • Green infrastructure and healthy public spaces.

Community Involvement & Collaboration

Transparency, regular forums, and real collaboration with District 4 residents and businesses.
