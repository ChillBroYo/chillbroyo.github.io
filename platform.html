Our Campaign Priorities

To bring integrity, accountability, equity and inclusion back to City Hall. To reduce social disparity, empower our community and instill in future generations that we are worthy.

My Mission Statement

  • I am committed to advocating for and helping build a San Diego where all communities can thrive, be housed, and live quality lives. My identities and lived experiences have enabled me to see our system and societies from the margins, and I hope to use that knowledge alongside communities and other responsive leaders to build a better District 4 and San Diego for all of us. I will bring integrity, accountability, equity and inclusion back to City Hall striving to reduce social and economic disparity and empower our community and future generations.

Community-Based Policing

  • Support community policing programs that assign consistent officers to neighborhoods, building trust, familiarity, and faster response times

Police Accountability & Transparency

  • Ensure officers receive regular de-escalation and cultural competency training, and reinforce the use of body-worn cameras to keep both residents and officers safe.

Safer Streets & Infrastructure

  • Invest in streetlights, traffic calming, and pedestrian safety.

Youth & Violence Prevention Programs

Fund mentorship, after-school programs, and job pathways to prevent crime before it starts

Rapid Response & Crisis Teams

Pair law enforcement with mental health professionals to respond to crises safely and humanely.
